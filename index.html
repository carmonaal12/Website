Who I am?

Hi! I'm passionate about 3D and videogame development, with experience in modeling, texturing, animation and game mechanics design. Currently, I am pursuing a higher degree in 3D Animation, games and interactive environments, where I have honed my skills in tools such as Maya, ZBrush, Unity, Nuke, Premiere, Photoshop, Illustrator and After Effects, among others.

My goal is to be part of creative and challenging projects that allow me to learn and grow in this industry, bringing my passion for texturing and interactive storytelling. I'm excited about working in a team and creating experiences that connect with people, in video games, animation projects, film, television... In short, everything related to audiovisual entertainment.

Explore my work, and don't hesitate to get in touch if you want to collaborate or just chat about creative projects!

My projects

3D Generalist

Throughout my training, I have gained experience in the field of 3D modeling, using professional tools such as Maya, 3ds Max and ZBrush. I have learned to create detailed 3D models, both for characters and environments, mastering advanced texturing and UV mapping techniques to achieve realistic and high quality finishes.

In addition, I have experience working with Nuke, where I have explored its compositing environment, integrations and workflows. I have learned how to perform complex compositing, combining 3D elements with live images to create coherent and quality visual effects. My focus in Nuke is on achieving clean and effective integrations, working with layers, effects and masks to enhance the visual narrative of each project.

Thanks to my knowledge in these tools, I can develop complete projects, from modeling and texturing to integration and compositing, ensuring optimal quality at every stage of the creative process.

Denial

This is a psychological horror game in which you will immerse yourself in Joshua's most traumatic and determining episodes of schizophrenia, which will make you discover a little more of his disorder and accept that "it is only the first stage of grief, the DENIAL".

Duties performed: This game is the most ambitious project I have worked on so far. With the help of Emma Fernández, Jairo Navalpotro, Pablo Torres, Natalia Penedo, Eva Valladolid, Unai Pons, Sergio Quesada and Adrián García; we have developed this narrative and audiovisual experience through the videogame environment. With my role as the only programmer as well as director of the videogame, I have developed my skills with the Unity graphics engine as well as my knowledge of C#, and above all team management and optimization.

Software used: Unity 3D | URP, Autodesk Maya, Adobe Phostoshop, Zbrush, Adobe Substance Painter, Adobe Audition

Ghoul Geyser

I have developed a 2D video game called Ghoul Geyser, a 2D wave-based game in which the player must face hordes of enemies that increase in difficulty with each round. The main feature of Ghoul Geyser is its focus on fast action and its constant wave dynamics, with increasing difficulty as the player progresses.

Duties performed: Videogame developed alone, where I designed the difficulty progression system, enemy programming and HUD using Unity and C#. This project allowed me to develop skills in system design and gameplay balancing.

SlotVerse

SlotVerse is a game developed for a Game Jam, where each match offers a unique experience. Players spin a slot machine to randomly combine a game mode, enemy type, and setting, generating a different game scenario every time. The result can be anything from a chaotic top-down shooter to a high-speed 2.5D racing challenge, each with its own mechanics and enemy behaviors.

Duties performed: Worked as 3D modeler and UI designer/programmer. I created in-game assets and designed the interface logic, ensuring clarity and consistency across randomly generated game modes.

Software used: Unity 3D | 2D, Photoshop, Autodesk Maya, Substance Painter.
